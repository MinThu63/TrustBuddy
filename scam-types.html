Common Scam Types in Singapore

Learn to recognise these scams and protect yourself and your loved ones.

👨‍👩‍👦

Family Impersonation

CRITICAL

Example: "Hi Mum, I dropped my phone. This is my new number. Can you send $500 to this account urgently?"

How it works: Scammer pretends to be your child with a "new number" and asks for urgent money transfer via PayNow or bank transfer.

🛡️ Protection: Call your child on their original number. Ask a personal question only they would know.

🏦

Fake Bank Alert (Phishing)

CRITICAL

Example: "[DBS] Unauthorized transaction of SGD 3,500 detected. Verify now: https://dbs-secure-login.com"

How it works: SMS with fake bank link steals your login credentials and OTP when you click and enter details.

🛡️ Protection: Banks NEVER send clickable links via SMS. Call the number on the back of your card.

👮

Government Impersonation

HIGH

Example: "This is the Singapore Police. Your bank account is linked to money laundering. Transfer your funds to this safe account."

How it works: Caller claims to be Police/MAS/ICA and pressures you to transfer savings to a "safe" account for "investigation."

🛡️ Protection: Police will NEVER ask you to transfer money. Hang up and call 999 if unsure.

💰

Investment Scam

CRITICAL

Example: "Guaranteed 20% returns per month! Join our exclusive Telegram group. I made $50,000 in one week!"

How it works: Victim joins a group, makes small investments with "returns," then invests big. Platform disappears with all funds.

🛡️ Protection: No legitimate investment guarantees high returns. Check MAS Investor Alert List.

💕

Love / Romance Scam

HIGH

Example: "I want to visit you in Singapore but need money for my plane ticket. I promise I'll pay you back."

How it works: Fake dating profile builds emotional connection over weeks, then fabricates emergencies requiring money.

🛡️ Protection: Never send money to someone you haven't met in person. Reverse image search their photos.

📦

Parcel Delivery Scam

MEDIUM

Example: "SingPost: Failed delivery attempt. Pay $2.50 to reschedule: http://singpost-pay.com"

How it works: Fake delivery SMS asks for small "fee" via link. Link steals credit card details and drains your account.

🛡️ Protection: SingPost/Ninja Van never ask for payment via SMS links. Use their official app only.

💼

Job Scam

HIGH

Example: "Earn $300/day liking YouTube videos from home! Pay $50 registration fee to start immediately."

How it works: Small tasks pay initially to build trust. Then victim is asked to "invest" to unlock higher pay. Money never returns.

🛡️ Protection: Legitimate jobs never ask you to pay money upfront. If you pay to earn, it's a scam.

🏛️

CPF / IRAS Tax Scam

HIGH

Example: "IRAS: Your tax return has an error. Click to verify: https://iras-refund-sg.com. You are owed $3,200."

How it works: Fake SMS impersonates CPF/IRAS. Link steals SingPass credentials or banking details.

🛡️ Protection: CPF and IRAS never send clickable links via SMS. Use SingPass app or official websites only.
